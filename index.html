Model Overview: Agency-First Workflow

In this architecture, job postings are controlled exclusively by the Agency admin. Candidates submit their applications and resumes, which are first reviewed, interviewed, and graded by **Recruiters**. Only high-scoring candidates are submitted to the **Client Portal** for final employer review.

1. Candidate Submission

Candidates upload their profile and CVs via the Candidate Portal. They see vacancies managed directly by the agency's executive team.

2. Recruiter Verification

Recruiters use the Recruiter Dashboard to screen files, conduct preliminary phone interviews, change application status, and add notes.

3. Secure Client Delivery

Employers log in to the Client Portal to request recruitment services, specify hiring parameters, and review curated shortlists of pre-screened talent.
